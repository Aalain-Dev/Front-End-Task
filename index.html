Our Mission Statement

Christian Power Equipment, Inc. is a manufacturer's representative - selling equipment and services in Northern California, Nevada and beyond. Our market focus is on the power generation, process, petrochemical, refining, food processing, water/wastewater and other heavy industries. We also provide comprehensive technical and engineering support, including inspections, troubleshooting and advisory services.

Our goal is to be a trusted and responsive resource for anyone seeking parts, service or new capital equipment. Please take advantage of our 30+ years of experience and inquire today.
